## Spacious and Stylish

The Legend A40 is the boat you book when a normal RIB won't cut it. Two cabins (yes, actual beds), a galley kitchen, a lounge that seats 12, and a deck layout designed for entertaining. It's not a ferry ride with snacks — it's a private venue on the water that happens to move.

## Important Notes

* Passengers have to remove their shoes, before embarkation.
* According to Greek law, all Yachts in Greece cannot moor directly on the beach. We choose the best points for swimming and snorkeling, the closest possible from the beach.
* Passengers, choosing a tailor made tour, have to respect captain's safety instructions. The Itinerary may be adjusted depending on weather conditions. 
* It is recommended to have with you sunglasses, sunhat, sunscreen and dry clothes.
* We suggest bringing your own towels as they are not always available on board.
