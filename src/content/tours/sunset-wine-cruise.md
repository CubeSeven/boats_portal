## Evening Magic on the Water

There is no better way to end a day in Skiathos than on the water. As the sun begins its descent, the air cools and the sea becomes a mirror. We take you away from the busy beach bars to a quiet spot on the western coast where the only sound is the water against the hull.

We include a curated selection of Greek wines and a board of traditional local snacks—olives, cheese, and paximadi—carefully selected to complement the experience.
