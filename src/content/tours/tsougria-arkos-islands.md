## Paradise on Your Doorstep

You don't have to sail far from Skiathos Town to find total paradise. Tsougria and Arkos are the gems of the south coast. Uninhabited and unspoiled, they offer some of the best swimming and snorkelling in the entire Sporades.

Our private boat allows you to visit the quietest parts of these islands, avoiding the large ferry boats that drop hundreds of people at once. We know the exact spots where the water is clearest and the sand is softest.
