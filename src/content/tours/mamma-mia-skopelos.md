## Abba, Blue Water, and Movie Magic

Skopelos became the fictional island of "Kalokairi" in 2008, and the world fell in love with its emerald forests and sapphire waters. Our private tour takes you directly to the heart of the action, bypassing the crowded bus routes.

The highlight of the day is undoubtedly the church of Agios Ioannis at Kastro. Perched on a 100-metre high rock, it is the site of the wedding finale. We anchor directly below, giving you the best vantage point before you start your climb.
