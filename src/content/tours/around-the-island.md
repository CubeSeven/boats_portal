Why choose one beach when you can see them all? Our "Around the Island" tour is for the explorers. We circumnavigate the entire coastline of Skiathos, covering over 30 nautical miles of world-class beaches, dramatic cliffs, and hidden islets.

From the rugged wild north to the sandy chic south, this tour gives you the full picture of our island. It's a long, fulfilling day of sailing, swimming, and discovery.
