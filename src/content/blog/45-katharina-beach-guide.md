## About Katharina Beach

Katharina is a tiny, secluded sandy beach on Skiathos's south coast, approximately 11 km southwest of Skiathos Town. It sits between Maratha and Koukounaries, hidden from the main road by a screen of trees. The beach is characterized by its tamarisk trees — salt-tolerant evergreens that provide dappled natural shade over the sand. It's small, non-organized, and wonderfully peaceful.

## Getting There

### By Land
A narrow, unmarked path leads to Katharina from the coast road between Maratha and Koukounaries. It's not on the bus route. The nearest bus stop is Stop 18 (Maratha) — a 10-minute walk along the coast.

### By Boat
The best way to visit. Katharina faces south into a small bay. From Skiathos Port, a 30HP rental boat reaches Katharina in approximately 35 minutes.

### Transfer
A transfer from Skiathos Town takes about 25 minutes. Cost: approximately €35–45.

## The Beach

Katharina is approximately 80 metres of sand — small, intimate, and beautifully shaded by tamarisk trees. The water is azure blue and shallow. There are no facilities — the only company you'll have is the sound of the sea, the cicadas, and the breeze in the tamarisk branches.

### Facilities
- **None at all** — no sunbeds, no umbrellas, no bar, no toilets
- Tamarisk trees provide natural dappled shade — one of the best natural-shade beaches on the island
- Bring everything you need

### The Tamarisk Trees
Tamarisk trees (known in Greek as "armyriki") are the defining feature of Katharina. They grow right up to the sand, their feathery branches creating a canopy of light, filtered shade. The contrast of the silvery-green foliage against the blue water is beautiful, and the shade is a genuine luxury on a non-organized beach.

## Boat Anchoring Tips

The small bay is sheltered and calm. Anchor in 3–4 metres on sand. Space for 1–2 boats.

### Nearby Beaches to Combine
- **Maratha** (500m east, 2 minutes by boat) — slightly larger, pine-backed beach with a taverna
- **Koukounaries** (1.5 km west, 5 minutes by boat) — the island's most famous beach
- **Troulos** (2 km east) — sheltered family beach

## What Sea Skiathos Offers

- **Self-drive no-license boat rental:** 35 minutes from port. A perfect quiet stop on the south-coast route.
- **Skippered day trip:** Your captain will drop you at Katharina for a private swim under the tamarisk trees.
- **Water taxi:** Direct transfers to this hidden cove.

## Best Time to Visit

- **Morning:** The tamarisk trees cast beautiful morning shade patterns on the sand.
- **June and September:** Quietest months. You'll likely be alone.
- **July–August:** Still quiet compared to neighbouring beaches. The tamarisk shade is a genuine asset in peak summer heat.
