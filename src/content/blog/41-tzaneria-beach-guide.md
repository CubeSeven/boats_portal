## About Tzaneria Beach

Tzaneria is a small, attractive sandy cove on Skiathos's south coast, approximately 7 km southwest of Skiathos Town. Between Kanapitsa and Vromolimnos, backed by trees, it's known for two things: its PADI dive centre and its well-organized, family-friendly setup. It's less busy than its neighbours, offering a more relaxed experience with the unique addition of scuba diving.

## Getting There

### By Land
- **Bus:** Stop 15 (between Kanapitsa and Vromolimnos). About 16 minutes from Skiathos Town.
- **Car/Scooter:** 12-minute drive. Limited roadside parking.
- **Walk:** 5 minutes from Kanapitsa along the coastal path.

### By Boat
Tzaneria faces south into a small cove. From Skiathos Port, a 30HP rental boat reaches Tzaneria in approximately 18–22 minutes.

### Water Taxi
A water taxi from Skiathos Town takes about 15 minutes. Cost: approximately €25–35.

## The Beach

Tzaneria is approximately 200 metres of golden sand with clear, calm water. The beach is fully organized and particularly popular with families due to the shallow water and protected cove shape.

### Facilities
- Sunbeds and umbrellas (€8–12 per set)
- PADI dive centre — one of the few on Skiathos
- Watersports (paddleboards, kayaks, pedal boats)
- Beach bar with food and drinks
- Showers and changing facilities

### The Dive Centre
Tzaneria's dive centre is its standout feature:
- PADI courses from Discover Scuba Diving (beginner, no experience needed) through to Advanced Open Water
- Guided dives to nearby reefs and underwater rock formations
- Equipment rental for certified divers
- Snorkelling gear rental

The underwater topography around Tzaneria is excellent for diving — rocky reefs, small caves, and diverse marine life including octopus, moray eels, grouper, and colourful wrasse.

## Boat Anchoring Tips

Anchor in 3–5 metres on sand in the cove. The cove is small — space for a few boats. Be mindful of dive boats coming and going.

### Nearby Beaches to Combine
- **Kanapitsa** (500m east, 2 minutes by boat) — larger watersports hub
- **Vromolimnos** (1 km west, 3 minutes by boat) — beach bars and watersports
- **Achladies** (2.5 km east) — larger family beach
- **Sklithri** (1.5 km east) — the taverna-on-the-sand dining beach

## What Skiathos Boats Offers

- **Self-drive no-license boat rental:** 18–22 minutes from port. Combine with dive centre activities.
- **Skippered south-coast tour:** Visit Tzaneria and neighbouring coves. We can coordinate with the dive centre for those who want to dive.
- **Water taxi:** Direct transfers.

## Best Time to Visit

- **June and September:** Best for diving — water is warm and visibility is excellent.
- **Morning:** The dive centre runs morning sessions (usually 9am–1pm). The beach is calm.
- **July–August:** Busy but manageable. The dive centre may be booked up — reserve in advance.
