## Skiathos Has Multiple Watersports Beaches — Not All Are Equal

Different beaches suit different activities. A calm lagoon is perfect for SUP but terrible for jet ski. A windy exposed beach is ideal for parasailing but no good for a wakeboard lesson. Here's the breakdown.

## Koukounaries — Best for Beginners & Families

Skiathos' most famous beach is also its best for first-timers. The shallow, calm lagoon area behind the main beach — separated from the open sea by a sandbar — is ideal for SUP, canoe, and beginner water skiing. You're protected from wind and swell, which means more time on your feet and less time falling in.

**Best for:** SUP, Canoe, Pedalos, Beginner water ski
**Not ideal for:** Jet ski (too shallow), Parasailing (calm air)
**Facilities:** ★★★★★ (sunbeds, restaurants, parking)

## Banana Beach — Best All-Rounder

Banana Beach (actually two beaches — Big and Little Banana, separated by a rocky headland) has the widest range of watersports in Skiathos and the best infrastructure for setting up a full day of activities.

**Best for:** Everything — jet ski, parasailing, banana boat, tubes, wake ski
**Entry point for:** Our jet ski and parasailing operations
**Facilities:** ★★★★☆ (sunbeds, taverna, parking)

## Vromolimnos — Best for Water Ski & Wakeboard

Vromolimnos is the only beach on Skiathos with a dedicated slalom water ski course — not a casual setup, but a properly marked course with buoys. The water is calm in the morning and deep enough for safe speeds.

**Best for:** Water ski, wakeboard, slalom skiing
**Our base:** We operate wakeboard and water ski lessons from here
**Facilities:** ★★★☆☆ (sunbeds, beachfront taverna)

## Kanapitsa — Best for Jet Ski (Serious)

The sheltered bay at Kanapitsa opens to the south — enough fetch for proper jet ski enjoyment on the open water, while the headland shelters you from the Meltemi wind that batters the north coast. Open-water jet ski tours depart from here daily, reaching speeds you can't safely hit in crowded coves.

**Best for:** Jet ski rental, RIB powerboat rental
**Our base:** Jet ski hire and self-drive boats operate from Kanapitsa
**Facilities:** ★★★☆☆
