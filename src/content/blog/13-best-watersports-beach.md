## Skiathos Has Multiple Watersports Beaches — Not All Are Equal

Different beaches suit different activities. A calm lagoon is perfect for SUP but terrible for jet ski. A windy exposed beach is ideal for parasailing but no good for a wakeboard lesson. Here's the breakdown.

## Koukounaries — Best for Beginners & Families

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Skiathos' most famous beach is also its best for first-timers. The shallow, calm lagoon area behind the main beach is ideal for SUP, canoe, and beginner water skiing.

**Best for:** SUP, Canoe, Pedalos, Beginner water ski
**Not ideal for:** Jet ski (too shallow), Parasailing (calm air)
**Facilities:** ★★★★★ (full range of operators, restaurants, parking)

## Banana Beach — Best All-Rounder

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Banana Beach (actually two beaches — Big and Little Banana) has the widest range of watersports in Skiathos, the most operators, and the best infrastructure.

**Best for:** Everything — jet ski, parasailing, banana boat, tubes, wake ski
**Entry point for:** Our jet ski and parasailing operations
**Facilities:** ★★★★☆ (multiple operators, decent taverna, sunbeds galore)

## Vromolimnos — Best for Water Ski & Wakeboard

**Best for:** Water ski, wakeboard, slalom skiing
**Our base:** We operate wakeboard and water ski lessons from here
