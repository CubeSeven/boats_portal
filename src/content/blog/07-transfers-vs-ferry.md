## Getting to Skiathos From the Mainland

Skiathos has its own airport, but many visitors arrive or depart via Volos — the closest mainland port, roughly 2.5 hours from Athens by car or train. From Volos, the options range from 2-hour slow ferries to a direct private transfer in under 90 minutes.

## Option 1: Conventional Ferry

The standard Volos–Skiathos ferry is the workhorse of the route — big, slow, and reliable. It takes approximately **2 hours 30 minutes** and runs 2–3 times daily in season.

- **Cost:** ~€18–22 per person
- **Vehicle:** Cars accepted
- **Comfort:** Deck seating, basic café
- **Luggage:** Unlimited
- **Booking:** hellenic-seaways.gr or ferryhopper.com

## Option 2: High-Speed Catamaran

The Flying Cat catamaran service from Volos cuts the journey to approximately **1 hour 30 minutes** — same seats as the ferry but half the time, which matters when you've just landed at Athens airport and you're counting the hours to the beach.

- **Cost:** ~€32–40 per person
- **Vehicle:** Passengers only
- **Comfort:** Air-conditioned seating
- **Runs:** June–September only

## Option 3: Flying Dolphin Hydrofoil

The classic Greek island hydrofoil — fast, noisy, and slightly terrifying in a fun way. These have been running the Aegean routes since the 1970s and the retro vibe is part of the appeal. Journey time approximately **1 hour 15 minutes**.

- **Cost:** ~€35–45 per person
- **Vehicle:** Passengers only
- **Runs:** Irregular schedule, check ahead

## Option 4: Private Transfer

Our private transfer service from Skiathos port to Volos harbour runs door-to-boat in approximately **75–90 minutes** depending on sea conditions. No ticket office, no timetable, no standing on a hot quay with your bags waiting for a delayed ferry.

- **Cost:** From €350 per boat (up to 8 passengers)
- **Departs:** Any time you choose, 24/7
- **Luggage:** Unlimited
- **Stops:** Optional — we can detour via Skopelos or a beach en route
- **Comfort:** Private, no crowds, no queues

## The Verdict

**For budget travel:** Take the conventional ferry. **For speed + comfort:** High-speed cat. **For groups or premium transfers:** Our transfer works out to less than €50 per person for groups of 6+, with the bonus of choosing your own schedule and making stops along the way.
