## The Most Iconic Boat Trip in the Aegean

When Mamma Mia filmed on location in 2007, Skiathos and Skopelos became instant icons of the Greek island dream. The clifftop chapel of Agios Ioannis — where Sophie and Sky famously wed — became one of the most photographed locations in Greece. And yes, you can reach it entirely by boat.

**Journey time:** ~50 minutes at moderate speed in the Compass 165 or Marinco RIB.

## Stop 1: Glysteri Bay (20 min from port)

A secluded pebble beach on Skiathos' eastern coast, rarely visited by tourists. Drop anchor, swim, snorkel. This is where we recommend your first proper stop.

## Stop 3: Agios Ioannis Chapel — The Mamma Mia Church (30 min from Skopelos Town)

The 215-step climb is worth every step. The tiny whitewashed chapel sits at the tip of a sea rock with sheer drops to the Aegean on three sides. Views are staggering.

**Practical info:** The chapel is small — arrive early or late to avoid queues. Swimming directly below is possible and excellent.
