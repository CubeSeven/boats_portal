## About Xenia Beach

Xenia is a charming sandy beach on Skiathos's southwestern coast, approximately 12 km from Skiathos Town. It sits just west of the famous Koukounaries beach and shares its pine-forest backdrop, but with a significantly quieter atmosphere. If Koukounaries feels overwhelming, Xenia offers the same golden sand and turquoise water with a fraction of the crowd.

## Getting There

### By Land
- **Bus:** Stop 25 (Agia Eleni) or Stop 26 (Koukounaries). About 30 minutes from Skiathos Town. The beach is a 5–10 minute walk west from Koukounaries along the coast.
- **Car/Scooter:** 20-minute drive. Limited parking.
- **Walk:** An easy 10-minute coastal walk from Koukounaries through the pine forest.

### By Boat
Xenia faces southwest with a sandy bottom — excellent for anchoring. From Skiathos Port, a 30HP rental boat reaches Xenia in approximately 40–45 minutes.

### Transfer
A transfer from Skiathos Town takes about 30 minutes. Cost: approximately €40–50.

## The Beach

Xenia is approximately 200 metres of soft golden sand with shallow, clear water. It's well-organized but low-key — sunbeds and umbrellas, a beach bar, but none of the noise or chaos of its more famous neighbour.

### Facilities
- Sunbeds and umbrellas (€8–12 per set)
- Beach bar serving drinks and light food
- Showers
- Pine forest behind providing natural shade
- No watersports (the quiet is the point)

### Why Choose Xenia Over Koukounaries
- Same sand, same water, same pine forest backdrop
- Significantly fewer people — even in peak season
- More relaxed atmosphere
- Still close enough to walk to Koukounaries for watersports or lunch if you want them

## Boat Anchoring Tips

The bay is open to the southwest. Anchor in 3–5 metres on sand. More space than Koukounaries, with fewer boats.

### Nearby Beaches to Combine
- **Koukounaries** (500m east, 2 minutes by boat or 10-minute walk) — for watersports, restaurants, and facilities
- **Banana** (1.5 km west, 5 minutes by boat) — party beach and watersports
- **Agia Eleni** (1 km west) — organized beach with nudist-friendly sections
- **Maratha** (3 km east) — quiet, pine-backed beach

## What Sea Skiathos Offers

- **Self-drive no-license boat rental:** 40–45 minutes from port. The perfect base for exploring the Koukounaries area without the crowds.
- **Skippered day trip:** Combine Xenia with Koukounaries and Banana. Enjoy the best of all worlds.
- **Water taxi:** Direct transfers. Privacy, no crowds, your own schedule.

## Best Time to Visit

- **Any time June–September:** Xenia is consistently quieter than Koukounaries.
- **Morning:** Calm water, peaceful atmosphere. You may hear the pine forest more than any other sound.
- **July–August:** Even in peak season, Xenia remains manageable. The contrast with Koukounaries next door is stark.
- **Late afternoon:** The light through the pine trees is beautiful.
