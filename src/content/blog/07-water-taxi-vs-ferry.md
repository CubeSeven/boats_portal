## Getting to Skiathos From the Mainland

Skiathos has its own airport, but many visitors arrive or depart via Volos — the closest mainland port, roughly 2.5 hours from Athens by car or train. From Volos, the options range from 2-hour slow ferries to a direct private water taxi in under 90 minutes.
