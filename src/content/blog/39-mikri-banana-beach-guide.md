## About Mikri Banana

Mikri Banana (Little Banana) is the smaller, quieter sibling of Banana Beach, located just 300 metres west along Skiathos's southwestern coast. It's approximately 14 km from Skiathos Town. While Banana is famous for parties and watersports, Mikri Banana has carved out a reputation as Skiathos's premier naturist beach — a well-organized, relaxed, and judgment-free zone where clothing is entirely optional.

## Getting There

### By Land
There is a footpath from Banana Beach (5-minute walk along the coast through the pine trees) or from the Koukounaries area. It's not signposted as prominently as other beaches, which contributes to its secluded feel. The path involves some uneven ground.

### By Boat
Mikri Banana faces southwest into a small cove adjacent to the larger Banana bay. From Skiathos Port, a 30HP rental boat reaches Mikri Banana in approximately 40–45 minutes. The approach is the same as Banana Beach — the smaller cove is just to the west.

### Water Taxi
A water taxi from Skiathos Town takes about 30 minutes. Cost: approximately €40–50.

## The Beach

Mikri Banana is approximately 100 metres of sand — small, intimate, and sheltered. The water is clear and shallow. The beach is well-organized despite its size, with quality sunbeds and a beach bar that's become something of a local institution.

### Facilities
- Sunbeds and umbrellas (€10–15 per set, high quality)
- One beach bar with full drinks menu and food service
- No watersports (the peace is the point)
- Showers

### The Atmosphere
Mikri Banana is defined by its relaxed, naturist-friendly ethos. There's no pressure and no judgment — some visitors are nude, others are not. The unspoken rule is respect and relaxation. The beach bar plays chilled music at a volume that doesn't dominate. It's the antithesis of Banana's high-energy party scene next door.

## Boat Anchoring Tips

The small cove offers some shelter. Anchor in 3–5 metres on sand. The cove is small — space is limited, especially in peak season. You can also anchor in the larger Banana bay and walk to Mikri Banana (5 minutes along the coastal path).

### Nearby Beaches to Combine
- **Banana Beach** (300m east, 2 minutes by boat or 5-minute walk) — for watersports, party atmosphere, and facilities
- **Koukounaries** (1.5 km east) — the island's most famous beach
- **Agia Eleni** (1 km west) — organized beach with a quiet western end
- **Xenia** (1.5 km east) — quiet beach near Koukounaries

## What Skiathos Boats Offers

- **Self-drive no-license boat rental:** Combine Mikri Banana with Banana and Koukounaries in a single south-coast day trip.
- **Skippered day trip:** Your captain will drop you at Mikri Banana and collect you when you're ready. Stress-free.
- **Water taxi:** Direct transfers. Privacy guaranteed.

## Best Time to Visit

- **June and September:** Best balance of weather, atmosphere, and space. The beach bar is open.
- **Weekdays:** Noticeably quieter than weekends.
- **Morning (before 11am):** Calmest water, most space, best sunbed availability.
- **July–August:** Popular but limited in size — the sunbeds fill quickly. Arrive before 10am or after 4pm.
