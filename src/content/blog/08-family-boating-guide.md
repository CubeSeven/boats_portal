## Can You Take Kids on a Rental Boat in Skiathos?

Absolutely — and it's one of the best family experiences in Greece. Children of all ages are welcome on our boats. We have life jackets in every size from infant to adult, and our briefings include specific guidance for parents.

## Age & Safety Guidelines

Greek maritime regulations cover child safety on recreational vessels, and we go beyond the minimum. Our safety policy:

- **Children under 3:** Must wear an approved infant life jacket at all times while underway
- **Children 3–12:** Must wear a life jacket while the boat is moving; can remove when anchored
- **Teenagers 13+:** Life jacket recommended while underway
- **Maximum engine speed with children under 8:** We recommend keeping to 15 knots or below

## Best Boats for Families

The **Olympic 450 (30HP)** is our top family pick. Its low deck, stable hull, and modest speed make it ideal for nervous parents. The 5-person capacity is perfect for two adults and two or three children.

For larger families (up to 10 people), the **Marinco RIB** is surprisingly good with kids — the inflatable sponsons are soft, the deck is spacious, and the onboard shade is excellent.

## Best Kid-Friendly Beaches by Boat

These are the beaches our own staff take their kids to:

1. **Koukounaries** — Shallow, calm lagoon behind the beach. Perfect for toddlers.
2. **Banana Beach** — Clear water, gentle gradient. Water sports available.
3. **Tsougria island** — A 25-minute trip to a sandy island. Feels like a proper adventure.
4. **Kanapitsa** — Organised beach with facilities, 10 min by boat.

## What to Pack

Beyond the obvious (sunscreen, hats, water), here's the list we send to families before their trip:

- High-factor waterproof sunscreen (SPF 50+)
- Rash vests / UV swimsuits for children
- Snorkelling masks (child sizes) — we provide these
- Plenty of drinking water
- Snacks and sandwiches
- Hat and polarised sunglasses for adults
- Anti-nausea bands if children are prone to motion sickness
- Small plastic bags for wet gear
