## About Plakes Beach

Plakes is the beach in Skiathos Town — a small, pebbled cove right in the heart of the main settlement, within easy walking distance of everything. It's not a destination beach in the traditional sense, but rather a convenient swimming spot for those staying in town, with a small harbour, surrounding cafes and tavernas, and a lively urban atmosphere.

## Location

Plakes is within Skiathos Town itself, a short walk from the main port, the Old Port, and the pedestrian shopping streets. It's the most accessible beach on the island — no bus, no car, no boat required. Just walk down from wherever you are in town.

## The Beach

Plakes is a modest pebble beach — approximately 100 metres — with clear water and a small boat harbour at one end. The beach is surrounded by cafes, tavernas, and shops, giving it a distinctly urban character. It's popular with locals and visitors who want a quick swim without leaving town.

### Facilities
- Cafes and tavernas directly adjacent
- Small boat harbour
- Sunbeds at some adjacent cafes (with purchase)
- Toilets in nearby cafes and restaurants
- No formal beach organization

### The Harbour
The small harbour at Plakes accommodates local fishing boats and small pleasure craft. It adds character and authenticity — you're swimming in the same water the fishermen work from.

## Boat Access

Since Plakes is in Skiathos Town, boat access is essentially returning to port. It's the starting and ending point for every boat trip on the island, not a destination in itself. However, its harbour facilities make it a convenient meeting point.

## What Sea Skiathos Offers

- **Our base is in Skiathos Port** — Plakes is a 5-minute walk from our location. Pick up your rental boat and return for a post-swim coffee at one of the harbour cafes.
- **Post-cruise refreshment:** Return from your boat trip, tie up, and walk straight to Plakes for a swim and a drink at the water's edge.

## Best Time to Visit

- **Early morning:** The town is quiet. The harbour is peaceful. A pre-breakfast swim.
- **Late afternoon:** Perfect after a day on the boat. Swim, then walk to a taverna for dinner.
- **Any time:** Convenience is everything — Plakes is always there.
