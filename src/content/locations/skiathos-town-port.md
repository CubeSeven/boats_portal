## The Heart of Skiathos Maritime Life

Our Old Port location is where the history of Skiathos meets modern maritime adventure. Situated at the edge of the town's vibrant waterfront, this base serves as our strategic command center for all complex logistics, including island-to-island transfers and full-day private tours.

Whether you are staying in the town and want a quick sunset cruise, or you've arrived by ferry and need an immediate water taxi to your villa, our Old Port kiosks are staffed from 08:00 until the last arrival of the night.

## Key Advantages of the Old Port Base

- **Immediate Access:** No need for taxis or buses if you are staying in Skiathos Town.
- **Late Logistics:** The only base operating 24/7 for urgent water taxi requests.
- **Town Amenities:** Close to supermarkets for stocking up your rental boat's cooler.
