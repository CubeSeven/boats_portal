## A Peaceful South Coast Departure

Kolios Beach sits between Platanias and Vromolimnos on the southern coast, offering a quieter alternative for those who prefer a relaxed atmosphere. The bay is naturally sheltered, providing calm waters that are ideal for first-time boat renters and families with children.

The beach itself is long and sandy, with plenty of space to prepare your gear before heading out. From Kolios, you are within easy reach of the entire southern coastline — from the secluded coves near Kanapitsa to the golden stretches towards Troulos.

## Why Choose Kolios Beach?

- **Tranquility:** A less crowded base, perfect for those seeking a peaceful start.
- **Sheltered Waters:** Calm departures even when the wind picks up elsewhere.
- **Central Location:** Positioned midway along the south coast for maximum exploration range.
