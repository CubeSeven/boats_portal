## Sheltered Waters & Secret Coves

Kanapitsa is favored by experienced boaters and families alike for its naturally sheltered position. Protected from the northern winds, the waters here are consistently calm, making departures and arrivals effortless even for first-time renters.

From Kanapitsa, you are less than 15 minutes away from some of the most beautiful hidden coves on the southern coast that are completely inaccessible by car. It is the tactical choice for those who want to maximize their time swimming and minimize their time navigating.

## Why Rent from Kanapitsa?

- **Shelter:** The calmest bay for beginner briefings and engine starts.
- **Proximity:** Closest base to the famous Tzaneria and Diamandi coves.
- **Parking:** Easy parking access near the beach, making it simple to unload your gear.
