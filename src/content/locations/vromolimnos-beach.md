## The Center of Performance

Vromolimnos Beach has a reputation among locals as the place where the best water skiers in the Sporades congregate. The geography of the bay creates flat, glassy water most mornings—essential for high-speed performance and learning.

Our base here is distinct. While we offer a full range of services, our Vromolimnos team is specialized in tow-sports. If you are looking to learn to wakeboard, or you want to shave seconds off your slalom times, this is where you need to be.

## Elite Training Facilities

At Vromolimnos, we provide professional-grade conditions:

- **Slalom Course:** Fully buoyed and maintained daily.
- **Coaching:** Radio-equipped helmets for real-time instruction while on the water.
- **Equipment:** The latest high-performance skis and boards from leading global brands.
