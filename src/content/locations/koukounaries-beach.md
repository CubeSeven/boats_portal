## Golden Sand & Adrenaline

Koukounaries is not just a beach—it's a landmark. Known for its crescent-shaped golden sand and the thick pine forest that meets the sea, it is the perfect playground for our most active guests.

From our base here, we operate the island's largest collection of watersports. Whether you want to fly high with parasailing, zip across the bay on a jet ski, or take a calm paddle on a SUP in the morning, Koukounaries offers the best infrastructure and water conditions.

## Available Activities at Koukounaries

Because of the sheltered nature of the bay, Koukounaries is our primary hub for:

- **Parasailing:** Best launch conditions on the island.
- **Jet Ski Rentals:** A wide, safe operating zone away from swimmers.
- **Family Fun:** Bananas, tubes, and large pedalos for the whole group.
