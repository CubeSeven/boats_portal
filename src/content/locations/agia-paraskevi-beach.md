## A Complete Beach Base

Agia Paraskevi is one of the most beloved beaches on the south coast, known for its fine golden sand and shallow, crystal-clear waters that gently deepen. It is a favorite among families and couples alike, offering the perfect blend of natural beauty and convenience.

Our base at Agia Paraskevi is a full-service operation — from boat rentals and watersports to private tours departing directly from the shore. Whether you want to jet ski across the bay, parasail above the coastline, or take a boat out to explore hidden coves, everything is available right here.

## What Makes Agia Paraskevi Special

- **Ideal Swimming:** Shallow, clear waters that are safe for all ages.
- **Full Facilities:** Sunbeds, beach bars, and tavernas within walking distance.
- **Perfect Location:** Nestled between Kolios and Platanias beaches, with easy access to the entire south coast.
