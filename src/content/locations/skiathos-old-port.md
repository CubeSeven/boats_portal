## The Heart of Skiathos Maritime Life

Our Old Port location is where the history of Skiathos meets modern maritime adventure. Right on the town's waterfront, we run all our complex logistics from here — island-to-island transfers, full-day private tours, and late-night transfer calls. If you are staying in the town and want a quick sunset cruise, or you've arrived by ferry and need an immediate transfer to your villa, our kiosks are staffed from 08:00 until the last boat is safely in.

## Key Advantages of the Old Port Base

- **Immediate Access:** No need for taxis or buses if you are staying in Skiathos Town.
- **Late Logistics:** The only base operating 24/7 for urgent transfer requests.
- **Town Amenities:** Close to supermarkets for stocking up your rental boat's cooler.
