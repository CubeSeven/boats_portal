# Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: April 2026

By using our website and services, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms and conditions.

## 1. Rental Requirements
- **No-License Boats**: Must be at least 18 years old. Valid ID or Passport required.
- **With-License Boats**: Must provide a valid speedboat license recognized by the Greek Port Authorities.
- **Security Deposit**: A refundable security deposit is required for all self-drive rentals.

## 2. Booking and Cancellation
- **Bookings**: All bookings are subject to availability.
- **Cancellations**: Cancellations made 48 hours prior to the rental will receive a full refund. Same-day cancellations or no-shows are non-refundable.
- **Weather**: If we cancel the rental due to unsafe weather conditions, you will receive a full refund or a rescheduled date.

## 3. Usage Rules
- All boats must stay within the designated navigation zones (1 nautical mile for no-license boats).
- Reckless driving or operating under the influence is strictly prohibited.
- Damages caused by negligence will be charged to the renter.

## 4. Safety
- Life jackets are provided and must be worn as instructed.
- A full safety briefing will be provided before departure; guests must attend and confirm understanding.

## 5. Limitation of Liability
Skiathos Boats is not responsible for any personal injuries or loss of property during the rental period, except where required by law.
