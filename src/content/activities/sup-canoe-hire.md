For those who prefer the sound of the sea to the sound of an engine, our SUP (Stand-Up Paddleboard) and Canoe rentals offer a peaceful way to see the Skiathos coastline. It's great exercise and the best way to get close to the nature of the island.

We provide everything you need for a safe and enjoyable paddle session. Our team will give you a quick lesson on technique and local weather conditions.
