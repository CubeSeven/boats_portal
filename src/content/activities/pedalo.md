Pedalo hire is the perfect way to enjoy a relaxed, slow-paced adventure on the water. Paddle at your own speed around the beautiful, calm bay of Koukounaries. It is extremely stable, easy to pedal, and requires absolutely no experience or briefings.

It is a massive hit for families with young children, especially our models that feature built-in waterslides. Cycle out, anchor in the bay, slide into the cool turquoise sea, and pedal back whenever you're ready.
