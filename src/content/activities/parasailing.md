Parasailing is the calmest way to experience high adrenaline. Our specialized winch-boats allow you to take off gently from the deck and land just as softly, all while being safely harnessed to a parachute.

From 100 meters above the sea, you'll see the entire coastline of Skiathos, the neighboring islands, and on clear days, all the way to Mt. Pelion on the mainland.

**Safety First:** We only operate parasailing in specific wind conditions. If the breeze is too strong or from the wrong direction, we will reschedule your flight for the safest possible window.
